Lesson 1: Cascading Style Sheets

CSS, which stands for Cascading Style Sheets, is a style sheet language used to style a markup language, most commonly HTML. It is fundamental to coding because it improves the appearances of websites, making them more dynamic and visually appealing.

Lesson 1 was basically just the basics of CSS, and it helped me understand how to use CSS. It wasn't that hard for me to understand. It helps in designing your webpage and make it look nicer and pleasing to the eyes.
